Rui Yuan

 

I am a final-year PhD student jointly at Meta AI (formerly Facebook AI Research) and Télécom Paris (PhD expected in Feb. 2023). My thesis dirctor is François Roueff (Télécom Paris) and my supervisors are Alessandro Lazaric (Meta AI) and Robert M. Gower (Flatiron Institute).

Research

My main research interest is reinforcement learning (RL) and large scale stochastic optimization for machine learning. In particular, I focus on developing a fundamental understanding of optimization methods applied in RL and designing new efficient practical optimization algorithms to solve RL problems.

I am already on the job market and open for a machine learning researcher position starting in early 2023.
