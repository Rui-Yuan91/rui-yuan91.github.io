I am a final-year PhD student jointly at Meta AI (formerly Facebook AI Research) and Télécom Paris (Defend my PhD on Mar. 17th, 2023). My thesis dirctor is François Roueff (Télécom Paris) and my supervisors are Alessandro Lazaric (Meta AI) and Robert M. Gower (Flatiron Institute).

Research

My main research interest is reinforcement learning (RL) and large scale stochastic optimization for machine learning. In particular, I focus on developing a fundamental understanding of optimization methods applied in RL and designing new efficient practical optimization algorithms to solve RL problems. Besides, I am passionate about deriving simple and elegant proofs in research. I am rigorous in checking every single detail of the problem and try to explain my research as clear as possible.

I am also a maths lover. Recently, I am interested in using transformers to solve Olympiad maths exercises and more general mathematics problems.

I am now active on the job market and open for a machine learning researcher position.
